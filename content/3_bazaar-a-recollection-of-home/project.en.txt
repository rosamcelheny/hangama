Title: Bazaar, A Recollection of Home

----

Color: #D94A4A

----

Description: This exhibition titled Bazaar, A recollection of Home presents a new body of work assembled in a monumental textile installation. I explored different topics such as the politics of globalization, consumer exchanges between the East and the West, and, more specifically, social and gender norms within Afghan bazaars.

----

Year: 2020
